About Us

Provision Hacks is a student-led hackathon, lasting for 48 hours of development! Provision Hacks is all about gathering students from around the world and giving attendees an opportunity to build prototypes and to provide many learning opportunities for the attendees. There are many modern problems today that require modern solutions, and so the attendees will get the opportunity to form teams, learn, create, test, and present a solution targeted towards solving worldwide issues. Ranging from issues such as COVID-19 to Mental Health and Socio-Economic issues, you are free to make anything your heart desires. It is for the attendees to decide and to develop a project that is intended towards helping with world-wide issues that persist throughout society today. Provision Hacks would love to provide help and support for the projects being developed. We hope that the attendees have the best time and experience at our Hackathon! Please make sure that you have checked our schedule. Also, make sure that you have joined both our discord and devpost!
